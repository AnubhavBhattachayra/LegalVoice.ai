## Getting Started

### Account Creation and Login

1. **Registration**: Visit the homepage and click "Sign Up" to create a new account.
   - Fill in your personal details, email, and password
   - Verify your email address through the verification link
   - Complete your profile with additional information

2. **Login**: Access your account through the login page.
   - Use your registered email and password
   - Enable two-factor authentication for additional security
   - Use "Forgot Password" option if needed to reset your credentials

### Navigation Basics

The application features a persistent navigation menu with access to:
- Dashboard: Your central hub for activities and analytics
- Documents: Access to your legal document storage
- Chat: AI-assisted legal consultation
- Lawyers: Directory of legal professionals
- Profile: Your account and personal settings

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard serves as your personalized hub, providing at-a-glance information:

1. **Analytics Section**:
   - Documents analyzed and created
   - Recent consultations
   - Upcoming appointments
   - Usage statistics

2. **Quick Actions**:
   - Upload a document for analysis
   - Create a new document
   - Start an AI chat session
   - Book a consultation

3. **Recent Activity**:
   - Recently viewed documents
   - Latest chat conversations
   - Recent lawyer interactions
   - System notifications

## Document Management

### Document Upload and Storage

1. **Upload Documents**:
   - Navigate to the Documents section
   - Click "Upload Document" button
   - Select file from your device or drag-and-drop
   - Add optional tags and descriptions

2. **Document Organization**:
   - Create folders for different legal categories
   - Apply tags for easier searching
   - Star important documents
   - Archive old documents

### Document Analysis

1. **AI-Powered Analysis**:
   - Select any document and click "Analyze"
   - The system will process the document using Gemini AI
   - View detailed analysis including document type, key points, and potential issues
   - Access recommendations based on the document content

2. **Document Comparison**:
   - Select multiple documents to compare
   - View side-by-side comparison highlighting differences
   - Get AI-generated insights on the variations

## AI Chat Assistant

### Starting a Chat Session

1. **Access the Chat Interface**:
   - Navigate to the Chat section from the main menu
   - Start a new conversation or continue an existing one
   - Optionally select relevant documents as context

2. **Asking Questions**:
   - Type your legal question in the message input
   - Use suggested questions for common inquiries
   - Upload relevant documents during the conversation
   - Receive AI-generated responses based on legal knowledge

### Document-Grounded Conversations

1. **Referencing Your Documents**:
   - Use the Document Context panel to select relevant documents
   - Ask questions specifically about uploaded documents
   - The AI will refer to the selected documents in its responses
   - Receive citations and references to specific sections

2. **Language Options**:
   - Switch between multiple Indian languages
   - Translate conversations using the translate feature
   - Receive explanations in your preferred language

## Legal Document Drafting

### Creating Legal Documents

1. **Start a Drafting Session**:
   - From the dashboard or documents page, select "Create Document"
   - Choose from available document types (affidavit, agreement, etc.)
   - Provide initial requirements for the document

2. **AI-Assisted Drafting**:
   - Answer questions prompted by the AI about document details
   - Review AI-generated draft sections
   - Make edits and request revisions
   - Finalize and download the completed document

### Using Form Templates

1. **Filling Legal Forms**:
   - Browse available form templates by category
   - Select appropriate form for your needs
   - Fill the form with guided assistance
   - Download the completed form in PDF format

## Finding Legal Professionals

### Lawyer Directory

1. **Browsing Professionals**:
   - Access the lawyer directory from the main menu
   - Filter by specialization, location, language, and ratings
   - View detailed profiles with expertise and experience
   - Read client reviews and ratings

2. **Booking Consultations**:
   - Select a lawyer and view their availability calendar
   - Choose a suitable time slot
   - Provide consultation details and relevant documents
   - Confirm booking and payment details

### Consultation Management

1. **Preparing for Consultations**:
   - Review upcoming consultation details
   - Upload relevant documents for the lawyer to review
   - Prepare questions and discussion points
   - Receive reminders before scheduled appointments

2. **Post-Consultation**:
   - Access consultation notes and recommendations
   - Rate and review your experience
   - View and download any documents shared by the lawyer
   - Schedule follow-up appointments if needed

## Tips for Optimal Usage

1. **Document Preparation**:
   - Scan documents clearly before uploading
   - Use PDF format for best results
   - Organize documents with descriptive names
   - Apply relevant tags for easier searching

2. **Effective AI Interactions**:
   - Be specific with your questions
   - Provide context for better responses
   - Use document references when available
   - Break down complex legal questions into smaller parts

3. **Privacy Best Practices**:
   - Regularly review shared documents
   - Understand which documents are accessible to lawyers
   - Use secure passwords and enable 2FA
   - Log out from shared devices
